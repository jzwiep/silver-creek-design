About Us

Silver Creek Design, born out of an entrepreneurial spirit, is a Vancouver, BC. based design and drafting firm. Owner Gerrit Zwiep has gathered over forty years of experience working for and hand in hand with some of Canada’s most astute Consulting and Engineering Companies. He has done both Brown and Green Field situations. He`s travelled as for north as Diavik, a Diamond Mine located in the North Slave Region of the Northwest Territories and has done major project work in the Domtar Pulp and Paper Mills in Espanola and Dryden, Ontario.

Gerrit has absorbed from the ground up, working and gaining experience in all the relevant industries like Pulp and Paper, Petrochemical, Oil and Gas, Mining and several Oil Sands Projects. He`s been a maintenance planner, scheduler, supervisor and has done critical path planning and analyses.

Silver Creek Design promises to be innovative and inventive, always thinking outside the box. They will provide a high quality and sustainable design solution to help the client achieve their intended goals. The company’s aim is to give the client their requirements as priority number one. Silver creek is passionate and professional with a “let’s get it done right the first time” attitude. One of their main principles and philosophies is to always to “keep it simple”.
